Price Log Change for Item

This solution provides a dynamic system to record item pricing changes in real time, ensuring transparency and a comprehensive audit trail in rapidly changing market conditions.

View More Details
Overview

The system is designed to log any pricing changes made to an item. It records details such as the timestamp of the change, the user who performed the update, and both the old and new prices, generating a comprehensive report for audit and analysis purposes.

Key Objectives
  • Record every change in item pricing without altering the live price.
  • Capture essential details: timestamp, user, old price, and new price.
  • Generate a detailed report for audit and pricing strategy review.
Detailed System Functionality

Change Logging:

  • When a price change is attempted, the system logs the current price and the proposed new price along with the date/time and user details.

Reporting:

  • A comprehensive report is generated that records all pricing changes, providing a clear audit trail and supporting data for pricing reviews.
Benefits
  • Ensures transparency by maintaining an accurate log of pricing changes.
  • Provides an audit trail that facilitates compliance and review processes.
  • Supports strategic decision-making through detailed historical pricing data.
  • Improves accountability by clearly identifying who made each pricing change.
