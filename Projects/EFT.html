EFT Customization

A tailored solution for financial operations, this project customizes EFT templates to meet the requirements of banks such as Mizuho, MUFG, SMBC, and HSBC, and generates payment files in XML or TXT format using BFO FreeMaker.

View More Details
Overview

This project focuses on generating EFT templates that comply with the standards of leading banks, including Mizuho, MUFG, SMBC, and HSBC. Payment files are generated in XML or TXT format using BFO FreeMaker.

Key Objectives
  • Customize EFT templates to meet specific bank requirements.
  • Generate payment files in XML or TXT format using BFO FreeMaker.
  • Include support for banks such as Mizuho, MUFG, SMBC, and HSBC.
Detailed System Functionality

Template Customization:

  • Design EFT templates that conform to the standards of each supported bank.
  • Utilize BFO FreeMaker to populate the templates with payment data.

File Generation:

  • Generate final payment files in XML or TXT format, ready for bank processing.
Benefits
  • Ensures compliance with the requirements of multiple leading banks.
  • Simplifies the generation of standardized EFT payment files.
  • Streamlines financial operations by automating template generation.
